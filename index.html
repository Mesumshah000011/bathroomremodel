Floating vanities make a room feel larger. They also look super sleek.

Matte black is everywhere—and for good reason. It looks sharp and adds contrast.

Plan Your Bathroom Remodel Like a Pro

Before you pick out tile samples, do a little prep work.

Set a Budget That Works

Decide what you want to spend, then add a bit extra for those surprise costs.

Average cost of a bathroom remodel in Alpharetta: $12,000 to $30,000, depending on size and finishes.

Pick a Contractor You Can Trust

Go with someone local who knows the area and has done a bathroom remodel Alpharett projects before.

Ask them:

  • How long will it take?
  • Do you offer warranties?
  • Can I see past work?

Design with Function in Mind

Looks matter. But so does how the space works.

Think about:

  • Storage needs
  • Lighting for tasks like shaving or makeup
  • How open or tight the room feels

Don't Forget About Ventilation

Bathrooms get steamy. Without good airflow, that can lead to mold. Make sure to install a high-quality fan that fits the room size.
